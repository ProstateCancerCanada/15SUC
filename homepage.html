What is Step Up?

Climb the tallest towers in Canada’s biggest cities with the Do it for Dads Step Up Challenge! Not only will you be racing up 5 skyscrapers, but you will be helping lead the fight against prostate cancer. But don’t do this vertical hurl alone; teams of five will be challenged to climb 5,000ft while raising a minimum of $5,000. Register today and throw down the gauntlet as you challenge your colleagues to race to the top!
